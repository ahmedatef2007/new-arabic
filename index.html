Let me Introduce Myself

Egyption singer and actor with a musical career spanning more than three decades

He incorporates various genres into his music , including classical Egyption music, nubian music , blues , jazz and reggae. His lyrics are noted both for their philosophical content and for their passionate social and political commentary

Birth name & Born

Mohamed Mounir born in 10 October 1954. His family is from Nubia, Southern Aswan, Egypt.

Associated with

The Wailers, Amira, Dalia, Anoushka, Hamid Alshairi, Hamid Baroudi

Early Life

Mounir spent most of his early childhood in the village of Manshyat Al Nubia, where he shared his fathers's interests in both music and politicis. He and his family were forced to relocate to cairo when his village was lost in the floods that followed the construction of the Aswan Dam.

Mounir's first such concert - 1975

Although the public were initially critical of mounir for performing in casual attire at a time where many Egyption singers were expected to were suits.

Mounir's first recorded Album-1977

After completing his military service, mounir released his debut solo album "Alemony Eneeki" on the sonar record label.

Mounir's large concert-2004

Mounir held a large concert at the pyramids, during which he was physically attacked by a drunken fan. Despite sustaining minor injuries, he continued his performance until the end of the concert.

Mounir's concert in Opera-2008

Mounir postponed his New Year's Eve concert at Cairo Opera House in solidarity with the palestinians suffering the effects of the Gaza War.
